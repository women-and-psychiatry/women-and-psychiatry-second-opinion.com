I am a medical doctor with a Master’s degree in Psychiatry (2024) and a clinical and research focus on neurodevelopmental and personality-related conditions in women. My goal is to combine evidence-based assessment with empathy — offering second opinions that bring both clarity and understanding.

  • Education: Medical Doctor, Master’s in Psychiatry (2024)
  • Academic work: Thesis on Genotype–phenotype correlations in Autism Spectrum Disorder (in collaboration with Oslo University)
  • Diagnostic experience: DIVA-5, ADOS-2, RAADS-R, AQ, CAT-Q, and structured clinical interviews
  • Areas of interest: ADHD, Autism Spectrum Disorder, trauma-related and affective disorders, and diagnostic overlap in women
  • Approach: Integrative and person-centered — understanding the story behind the symptoms

My clinical and academic work are guided by one principle: that diagnostic clarity is not only a technical goal, but a form of validation — especially for those who have long felt unseen.
