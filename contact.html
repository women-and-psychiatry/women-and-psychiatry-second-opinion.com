Before you write

The second opinion is a written, educational, clinically reasoned perspective. It does not replace an in-person psychiatric assessment, ongoing care, or emergency services.

Good to know:

  • No AI is used in the assessment — it’s a medical review.
  • I read each message carefully and respond with clear reasoning.
  • If your case fits better with counseling, I’ll say so transparently.

Emergencies: If you are in immediate danger or considering self-harm, contact your local emergency number or go to the nearest emergency department.
